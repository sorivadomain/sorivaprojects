About Us

About Us

Manus Dei Secondary School is a co-educational Roman Catholic boarding school located in Kibwigwa, Kigoma, Tanzania. Founded under the guidance of Father Ferdinand Barugize, our school combines academic excellence with strong moral and spiritual formation.

Academic Achievement

We are recognized for consistently delivering high performance in national examinations, both in science and arts subjects. Over recent years, a significant proportion of our students have earned Division I and II results in CSEE.

Our Catholic Identity

As part of the diocesan education system, we are committed to nurturing not only the minds but also the hearts and souls of our learners. Our educational approach is deeply rooted in Catholic values, emphasizing discipline, faith, service, and love for our neighbors.
